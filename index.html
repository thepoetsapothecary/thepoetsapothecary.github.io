About

The same hands behind everything

Maker, poet, natural mystic. I have been creating things with my hands my entire life. Henna was just where you found me first.

I'm Giocchina Marie — independent creative practitioner rooted on Martha's Vineyard. My work spans organic henna, handmade body care, botanical teas, fiber arts, handmade paper, candles, sculpture, and bookkeeping for the kinds of small businesses I believe in.

I am also a grower — working toward a farm practice where what I grow, what I make, and what I offer are one continuous living thing. The botanicals in my teas, the herbs in my body care, the flowers pressed into my paper — all from the same earth.

Everything I make carries the same quality: slowly, deliberately, and with great care for what things are made of and what they mean.

Organic ingredients — always. No shortcuts on what touches your skin or your body.
Low-waste by intention — upcycled, reclaimed, diverted from landfill where possible.
Rooted in place — grown, made, and offered on Martha's Vineyard (Noepe).
Values-aligned — I work with people doing meaningful things.
"I make things with my hands and my whole heart. Not defined by a single medium — defined by a way of moving through the world."
— Giocchina Marie

Henna & Intention
Come sit with me. We begin with a moment of stillness — a chance to ask yourself what you're carrying and what you'd like to call in. Then I blend paste by hand and place a design on your skin while we talk, or sit quietly, or both. You'll leave with henna that lasts 1–2 weeks, an intention card, and the particular fullness that comes from being seen and made something beautiful.
