About

The same hands behind everything

Maker, poet, natural mystic. I have been creating things with my hands my entire life. Henna was just where you found me first.

I'm Giocchina Marie — independent creative practitioner rooted on Martha's Vineyard. My work spans organic henna, word-smithing, joyful craft and bookkeeping for the kinds of small businesses I believe in.

Everything I make carries the same intention: to bring joy.

Organic ingredients — always. No shortcuts on what touches your skin.
Low-waste by intention — upcycled, reclaimed, diverted from landfill where possible.
Rooted in place — grown, made, and offered on Martha's Vineyard (Noepe).
Values-aligned — I work with people doing meaningful things.
"I can't stop making things because it grounds me in the present moment that is always trying to escape me."
— Giocchina Marie

On Sourcing
The henna I use is organic powder sourced directly from a small family farm in Rajasthan, India — the historic heart of henna cultivation. I order from the farmer directly, maintaining a personal relationship with the source of every batch I blend. This is rare. It matters to me.

Henna & Intention
Come sit with me. We begin with a moment of stillness — a chance to ask yourself what you're carrying and what you'd like to call in. Then I blend paste by hand and place a design on your skin while we talk, or sit quietly, or both. You'll leave with henna that lasts 1–2 weeks, an intention card, and the particular fullness that comes from being seen and made something beautiful.
