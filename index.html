About

The same hands
behind everything

I'm Giocchina Marie — maker, poet, practitioner. I have been creating things with my hands my entire life. Henna was just where you found me first.

My work spans organic henna, fiber arts, body care, handmade paper, candles, sculpture, and bookkeeping for the kinds of small businesses I believe in. Everything I make or do carries the same quality: slowly, deliberately, and with great care for what things are made of and what they mean.

I am also a spoken word poet, a spiritual guide in the making, and the dreamer behind The Poet's Apothecary — a world where making things is medicine, and everyone gets to be the maker.

Organic ingredients — always. No shortcuts on what touches your skin.
Low-waste by intention — upcycled, reclaimed, diverted from landfill where possible.
Small by design — intimate experiences over mass production.
Values-aligned — I work with people doing meaningful things.
"I make things with my hands and my whole heart. Not defined by a single medium — defined by a way of moving through the world."
— Giocchina Marie

Henna & Body Art

Organic henna,
blended with intention

Every batch of paste is blended by hand using entirely organic ingredients — and something harder to name. Henna requires 24–48 hours of dye release time before it's ready to apply, so each batch is prepared fresh ahead of your appointment, then carefully stored to preserve its quality.

Available for private sessions, intimate gatherings, ceremonies, and events. I am mobile — I come to you on Martha's Vineyard, and remote consultation is available for planning.
